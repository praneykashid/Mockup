## 🔧 How to Use Mockup

Mockup offers a clean and straightforward interface. Here’s how to get started:

- **Open the Application**  
  Find the Mockup icon on your desktop or in the Start menu and double-click to open.

- **Create a New Design**  
  Click **New Project** or use the menu to start a blank canvas. You can choose the size and type of your design.

- **Add Elements**  
  Use the tools on the left side to add shapes, text, and images. Drag and drop items where you want them.

- **Edit Properties**  
  Click any element to change its color, size, or font using the options on the right panel.

- **Save Your Work**  
  Click **File**, then **Save** to keep your design on your computer. You can save it as a project file and reopen it later.

- **Export Your Design**  
  When your design is ready, use **Export** to save it as an image or PDF. This makes it easy to share or print.

## 🛑 Troubleshooting

If you run into problems, try these steps:

- **Installation Issues**: Check if your Windows is updated. Restart your PC and try again.  
- **App Won’t Open**: Make sure you installed it with administrator permissions.  
- **Design Tools Not Working**: Restart the app and open your project again.  
- **Saving Fails**: Confirm you have write permissions in the folder where you save.

For further help, you can visit the download page or look for help sections inside the app.
